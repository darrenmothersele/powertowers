WELCOME TO POWER TOWERS LIMITED

At Power Towers Limited we design and manufacture our range of low-level powered access products 100% in the UK.

We produce innovative, simple yet high specification and high quality products, specialising in the low-level access sector

Our products are simple, safe, easy and efficient to use. They can dramatically reduce working hours when compared with mechanical manual alternatives and represent excellent value for money.

All products comply with the relevant European Machineries Directives and are CE marked to EN280. They are all third party approved by SGS International.
